Pendle

Track what matters most

App Store Google Play

Many assume people can easily process text, numbers, and time, but that’s not true for everyone. As a visual thinker with dyslexia and difficulty remembering numbers, recalling time spans and durations is especially hard. That means things like “How long has it been since…?” or “When did I last…?” create unnecessary friction and mental load, even when the information should be simple and meaningful.

The answer is pendle.it — an app built to track what truly matters without forcing you to memorize numbers or interpret complex dashboards. Instead of drowning you in stats, pendle.it turns important moments, milestones, and personal metrics into intuitive, visual timelines and signals. It gives you clarity at a glance, helping you instantly understand how long it’s been, what deserves your attention, and what truly matters most.
